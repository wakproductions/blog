Here's a little project I did for fun to demonstrate a little Javascript single page app running on an AWS/Docker/Ruby on Rails stack.

US Treasury Yield Curve

The term structure of interest rates are an important barometer for the economy. When the yield curve flattens it's often a good predictor of economic slowdown and subsequent decline in the stock market. Play around with it and see what the interest rates have looked like historically!
